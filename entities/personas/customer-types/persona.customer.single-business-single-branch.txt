BUSINESS PROFILE PAGE FEATURES & UAT REQUIREMENTS

=== 1. EQUIPMENT & MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE ===
Profile section: "My Equipment & Maintenance"
- Display list of critical business assets requiring regular service
- For each asset:
  * Equipment name and type (e.g., "Diesel Generator 15KVA", "HVAC Unit")
  * Equipment manufacturer and model
  * Serial number (for warranty/compliance tracking)
  * Current status (operational, scheduled maintenance, under repair)
  * Last service date and agent who serviced it
  * Next scheduled maintenance date (if applicable)
  * Service history link (shows all past services on this equipment)
- Validation Points:
  * Equipment list is specific to this business site
  * Maintenance dates are tracked separately per asset
  * Service history for each asset is complete and accurate
  * Upcoming maintenance alerts are generated before due dates

=== 2. SERVICE HISTORY BY ASSET & CATEGORY ===
Profile section: "My Service History"
- Two view modes:
  a) By Asset: All services grouped by equipment (e.g., "Generator 15KVA" → [Service 1, Service 2, ...])
  b) By Category: All services grouped by service type (e.g., "Mechanical" → [Service 1, Service 2, ...])
- For each service entry:
  * Service date and duration (hours on-site)
  * Service category and type (maintenance, repair, emergency)
  * Agent name and specialty
  * Work description (from service call notes)
  * Downtime impact (if any): "Equipment was offline for X hours"
  * Final cost and payment status
- Validation Points:
  * Service dates match invoice dates
  * Equipment asset is correctly linked to each service
  * Service category matches agent specialty
  * Cost figures match finalized invoices
  * Downtime is tracked and reported accurately

=== 3. QUOTES, INVOICES & FINANCIAL STATEMENT ===
Profile section: "My Quotes, Invoices & Statement"
- Three subsections (same as residential, but with business context):
  a) Pending Quotes: Services requested but not yet quoted
     - Shows: Service request date, equipment/category, agent assigned, time elapsed
     - Validation: Critical services have SLA flags (e.g., "Generator repair – 24-hour response expected")
  b) Outstanding Quotes: Quotes provided, awaiting business approval
     - Shows: Quote date, equipment, agent, quote amount, quote expiry date, approval required indicator
     - Validation: Business authority checks (approver role may be required for quotes above threshold)
  c) Invoice History: Completed services with invoices
     - Shows: Invoice date, invoice number, equipment/category, agent, amount, VAT breakdown, payment date
     - Grouped by month or financial quarter
     - Validation: All invoices have VAT included/excluded clearly marked
- Financial Statement:
  * Total spent YTD (year-to-date)
  * Total spent by equipment (to track asset lifecycle costs)
  * Total spent by category (to identify high-cost service areas)
  * Total pending quotes (sum of outstanding quote amounts)
  * Average cost per service by category
  * Cost trend (YTD vs. prior year, if data available)
  * Payment compliance (on-time payment % for business credit purposes)
  * VAT total (separate line for tax reconciliation)

=== 4. SERVICE PROVIDER RELATIONSHIPS & PERFORMANCE ===
Profile section: "My Service Providers & Performance"
- Display list of agents/contractors business has worked with, ranked by frequency
- For each provider:
  * Agent/contractor name and specialty
  * Total services completed for this site
  * Overall rating (star average from business reviews)
  * Most recent review and rating
  * Last service date
  * Response time average (if available, e.g., "avg 4 hours from call to arrival")
  * Equipment specialization (e.g., "Generator specialist", "HVAC certified")
  * Contract/preferred vendor status (if business has formal agreement)
  * Action buttons: "Update Review", "View Performance History", "Contact"
- Validation Points:
  * Frequency counts match service history
  * Ratings are from business-authorized reviewers only
  * Response times are calculated from service call log timestamps
  * Preferred vendor status is accurately maintained

=== 5. BUSINESS REVIEW WORKFLOW ===
Interaction: Business manager/approver reviews service provider after service completion
- Pre-conditions:
  * Service is marked "Completed" and invoice is finalized
  * Approver role has been assigned to the business contact
  * At least 1 business day has elapsed since service completion
- Review Modal/Page:
  1. Information display:
     - Equipment serviced, service date, final invoice amount
     - Service notes (work performed)
  2. Rating section:
     - Star rating (1-5, with optional half-stars)
     - Category ratings (optional, e.g., Timeliness, Quality, Communication)
  3. Comment section:
     - Text area for detailed feedback (max 500 chars)
     - Examples: "Excellent response to emergency call, professional work"
  4. Compliance & follow-up:
     - Checkbox: "Would you recommend this provider for future services?" (Y/N)
     - Checkbox: "Should this provider be added to preferred vendor list?" (Y/N)
  5. Submit & Workflow:
     - On Submit: Review is saved, timestamp recorded, agent rating recalculated
     - Confirmation: "Review submitted. Provider rating updated."
- Validation Points:
  * Only authorized business contacts can submit reviews
  * One review per service (updates do not duplicate)
  * Review text is sanitized
  * Agent rating updates immediately
  * Preferred vendor flag propagates to vendor management system (if exists)

=== 6. COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY TRACKING ===
Profile section: "Compliance & Warranties" (optional, for business readiness)
- Checkpoints:

- Alerts for:

- Validation Points:
  * Warranty dates are tracked and enforced
