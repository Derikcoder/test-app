PROFILE PAGE FEATURES & UAT REQUIREMENTS

=== 1. SERVICE HISTORY (CATEGORICAL VIEW) ===
Profile section: "My Service History"
- Display all completed service calls, organized by service category (Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, Mechanical, etc.)
- Each category shows:
  * Count of completed services in that category
  * Most recent service date in that category
  * Expandable list of all service calls (dates, agent, cost, brief description)
- Validation Points:
  * Service categories match the agent specializations that worked on the property
  * Service dates and agent names are accurate
  * Costs match the finalized invoices
  * Completed services do not appear in pending/outstanding sections

=== 2. QUOTES & INVOICES (CATEGORICAL + STATEMENT) ===
Profile section: "My Quotes, Invoices & Statement"
- Three subsections:
  a) Pending Quotes: Services requested but not yet quoted
     - Shows: Service request date, service category, agent assigned, time elapsed
     - Validation: No quote exists yet; customer can see request is in progress
  b) Outstanding Quotes: Quotes provided, awaiting customer acceptance
     - Shows: Quote date, service category, agent, quote amount, quote expiry date
     - Validation: Quote exists; customer has not yet accepted; expiry date is enforced
  c) Invoice History: Completed services with invoices
     - Shows: Invoice date, service category, agent, invoice number, amount paid, payment date
     - Grouped by month or year
     - Validation: Invoices match completed service calls; payment status is accurate
- Total Statement at Bottom:
  * Total spent YTD (year-to-date)
  * Total pending quotes (sum of outstanding quote amounts)
  * Average service cost by category
  * Payment compliance (e.g., "100% on-time payments")

=== 3. FREQUENT SERVICE PROVIDERS & REVIEWS ===
Profile section: "My Service Providers & Ratings"
- Display list of agents customer has worked with, ranked by frequency (most frequent at top)
- For each agent:
  * Agent name and specialty/category
  * Total services completed (count)
  * Overall rating (star average from customer reviews)
  * Most recent review (date, rating, brief text excerpt)
  * Last service date
  * Next action button: "Update Review" or "Write Review" (depending on whether review exists post-service)
- Validation Points:
  * Agent list only includes agents who completed work for this customer
  * Frequency count matches service history
  * Ratings come from actual customer reviews; no review = no rating or "no rating yet"
  * Most recent review is the latest one posted after service completion

=== 4. REVIEW UPDATE WORKFLOW ===
Interaction: Customer clicks "Update Review" after service completion
- Pre-conditions:
  * Service call is marked as "Completed"
  * Service call has a finalized invoice
  * At least 1 review period has elapsed since service completion (optional: 24-48 hour buffer)
- Workflow:
  1. Modal/Page opens showing:
     - Agent name, service category, service date, final invoice amount
     - Star rating selector (1-5 stars, default to previous rating if exists)
     - Text area for review comment (max 500 chars)
     - Submit and Cancel buttons
  2. On Submit:
     - Review is saved to the service call record
     - Timestamp is recorded
     - Agent's overall rating is recalculated
     - Customer sees confirmation message: "Review submitted successfully"
  3. On Cancel:
     - No changes; modal closes
- Validation Points:
  * Customer can only review their own services
  * Only one review per service call (update overwrites previous, not creates duplicate)
  * Review text is sanitized (no SQL injection, XSS protection)
  * Agent's overall rating recalculates immediately
  * Review is visible on customer's profile and (if configured) visible to agent
